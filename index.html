The History of the Campbell Cup

Named in honor of Greg Campbell who taught for years at Davidsen. Greg LOVED fantasy sports! He tragically passed away very unexpectedly right before the season in 2021 and left behind a wife and two little girls. He was the nicest guy any of us who knew him ever met and I miss him a lot. To honor him, we renamed the league in his memory. I know he's smiling down from heaven and cheering for his Steelers.
